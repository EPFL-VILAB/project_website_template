(Under Development. Please check after March 5)

We provide a demo where you can upload (or use a preselected image) and visualize the outputs of different consistency-trained networks. The pretrained consistency models can be downloaded here.

Our networks were trained on 3 million images of varied indoor scenes. You can see the statistics of the training set here. If your query image severly deviates from these statistics, the performance is expected to degrade. Processing usually takes around 30 seconds.
